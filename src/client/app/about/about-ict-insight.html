ITC Insight is a monthly supplement to ITWeb Brainstorm magazine. Presented in a concise, easy to-read, dynamic and visually appealing format, the supplement presents the month's key company news, news analysis, thought leadership pieces, ICT job and events listings.

Each issue brings a summary of a recent ITWeb survey results, giving readers insights into the technology adoption trends and investment strategies in the local market - from business intelligence, to cloud computing and virtualisation, to security and mobility.

Editorial mission and content: Providing a concise, intelligent digest of solution provider and vendor news and developments, key ICT events, ITWeb's business technology survey results, and opinion pieces by people in the know.
