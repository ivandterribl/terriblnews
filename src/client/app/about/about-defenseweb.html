defenceWeb aims to be to the defence and civil security industry what ITWeb is to the ICT world. It has the ambition of becoming the preferred provider of news, insight and facts to the defence and security community and the public at large.

As a news service, it brings readers up-to-date self-generated reports, opinion and insight.
As a platform, it encourages players in the defence and security community to address each other and the broader public in their own words by means of its Virtual Press Office.
As a research port-of-call, it offers comprehensive coverage and linkage to security and defence sector reporting in Africa's media and academic publications.
As a public educator, it provides detailed open source files and data on defence and security issues as well as African militaries.
