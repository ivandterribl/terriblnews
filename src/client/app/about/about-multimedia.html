ITWeb Multimedia Department

Since its online debut in April 2010, the ITWeb Multimedia Department has grown exponentially and now publishes regular videos with numbers recorded as high as 113 000 views.

Evolving technology demands click-quick access to current news content. Professionals in the technology sector rely on ITWeb for their daily debriefing on all the latest ICT news. As the demand for video has grown over the past few years, ITWeb's video division has expanded to accommodate the migration to an audio-visual platform.

Spanning a wide variety of topics, the ITWeb Multimedia Department encompasses various categories and has adopted different styles to engage the targeted audience.

The video categories comprise:

News: the inside-scoop on current news affairs, as well as an update on all the latest trends and events.
Events: coverage of ITWeb's events and executive forums, providing highlights from the events, as well as interviews with world-renowned key speakers and industry professionals.
Interviews: opinion pieces with industry professionals, allowing in-depth analysis and discussion of initiatives and trends.
Industry videos: an insider's look at the inception, functioning and future development of companies, services and solutions in the technology industry.
Reviews: demonstrating the functions and capabilities of new gadgets and devices.
Adverts: showcasing the new offerings from service and solution providers in the ICT sector.

ITWeb Multimedia also produces regular content for ITWeb Africa, DefenceWeb, Brainstorm and Bytesize Digital.
